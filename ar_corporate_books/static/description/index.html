Argentine Corporate and Accounting Books

Manage corporate and accounting books according to Argentine regulations

Key Features

Corporate Books Management

Manage all types of corporate books as required by Argentine regulations:

  • Board Minutes Books
  • Shareholder Minutes Books
  • Shareholder Register Books
  • Attendance Books
  • Inventory Books
  • Journal Books
  • Ledger Books

Meeting Minutes

Record and manage meeting minutes with comprehensive details:

  • Board of Directors meetings
  • Annual shareholder meetings
  • Extraordinary shareholder meetings
  • Meeting attendees tracking
  • Signature recording and validation
  • Automatic PDF generation

Shareholder Registry

Maintain a complete and detailed shareholder registry:

  • Shareholder details and identification
  • Shares tracking and percentage calculation
  • Share transaction history
  • Shareholder certificates generation
  • Integrated with contacts and partners

Assembly Attendance

Track shareholder attendance at meetings:

  • Multiple attendance types (in-person, proxy, remote)
  • Share representation tracking
  • Automatic quorum calculation
  • Attendance certificates
  • Integration with meeting minutes

Module Benefits

Regulatory Compliance

Designed specifically to meet Argentine corporate and accounting regulatory requirements.

Complete Traceability

Keep track of all corporate changes, shareholders movements, and meeting decisions with proper documentation.

Professional Documentation

Generate professional and legally compliant certificates, reports, and official documents.

Technical Features

Full Integration

Seamlessly integrated with other Odoo modules like Accounting, Contacts, and Argentine Localization.

Security & Permissions

Comprehensive security groups and access rights to protect sensitive corporate information.

Document Management

Built-in attachment management for storing supporting documents for corporate actions.
